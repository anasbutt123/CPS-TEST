🚀 OUR STORY
About Us

We are gamers, developers, and performance enthusiasts dedicated to the art of the click.

Founded in 2024, our mission has always been simple: to build the world's most accurate, responsive, and aesthetically pleasing clicking speed measurement tools. We realized that many existing tools on the market were either outdated, inaccurate, or lacked the premium experience that modern competitive gamers deserve.

Why Accuracy Matters to Us

In the world of professional Minecraft PvP, speedrunning, and competitive clicker games, a single millisecond can be the difference between victory and defeat. That's why we built our engine using the high-resolution performance.now() API, ensuring that every single hardware interrupt is registered and calculated with 100% precision. We don't just count clicks; we measure human potential.

Built for the Community

Whether you're mastering the Jitter Click, practicing your Butterfly technique, or pushing your Spacebar speed to the limit, we've designed our tools to provide real-time, actionable feedback. Our endurance charts and interval tracking are features inspired directly by feedback from the competitive gaming community.

We are constantly evolving, adding new modes, and optimizing our algorithms to stay ahead of the curve. Thank you for making us your trusted partner in your journey to become a god-tier clicker.
