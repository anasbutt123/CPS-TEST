Right Click Test — Measure Your Right-Click Speed

Welcome to the most accurate right click test online. This tool measures how many times you can right-click per second using the same high-resolution timing used in our main CPS test. The context menu is suppressed inside the test area so every right-click is registered cleanly as a data point.

What is a Right Click Test?

A right click test measures your right-click speed in clicks per second (CPS). While most click speed tests focus on the left mouse button, right-click speed is equally important for gamers — especially in strategy games, MMORPGs, and survival games where right-clicking triggers critical actions like placing blocks, using items, or casting abilities.

How to Improve Your Right-Click Speed

  1. Use Your Ring Finger: Many players rest their ring finger on the right button. Switching to your middle finger for right-clicking can significantly increase speed.
  2. Adjust Your Grip: A claw grip allows faster individual finger actuation compared to a palm grip, improving right-click CPS.
  3. Warm Up: Warm up with the 1-second burst test before going for a full 5 or 10-second run.
  4. Consistent Practice: Use this right click test daily. Even 5 minutes of practice can improve your speed by 1–2 CPS over a week.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a right click test?
A right click test measures how many times you can right-click per second (CPS). It uses the same timing accuracy as a regular CPS test but registers only right mouse button clicks.
What is a good right click CPS score?
A good right click CPS is typically 5–8 for casual users. Competitive gamers can reach 8–12 CPS right clicking.
How is right click speed tested?
Our right click test uses high-resolution JavaScript event listeners that capture right mouse button clicks with millisecond accuracy using the performance.now() API.
